AI4PH Summer Institute Applications OPEN

The Artificial Intelligence for Public Health (AI4PH) Summer Institute will be held in Calgary, Alberta in 2024. Successful applicants will have the opportunity to expand their skills and knowledge related to the application of AI innovations in public health research and practice while exploring the potential impact of AI technologies on the widening or narrowing of health inequities.
This year’s theme will be “Introduction to image analysis and applications to public health”, with methods and examples organized around this topic. The application deadline for this has been extended to Friday, Apr. 5.

Accessing Health Data for Research in Alberta
The Centre for Health Informatics opened its doors to advance and innovate health informatics for Precision Health in the Cumming School of Medicine through timely data access, data analytics, linkage and coding. Through partnerships with AHS and other external collaborators, CHI is able to facilitate access to high quality, relevant data that can be used to support research and drive innovations.
Join us at the Centre for Health Informatics for an in-depth chat about how to access health data for research in Alberta.
