Dr. Ewan Affleck is coming to CHI

Dr. Ewan Affleck is a Senior Medical Advisor - Health Informatics, College of Physicians and Surgeons of Alberta, and is the past Chief Medical Information Officer of the Northwest Territories. A nationally recognized health data expert, he pioneered the implementation of an enterprise electronic medical record system in the Northwest Territories that is unprecedented in Canada in its level of integration. He served as a member of the Expert Advisory Group of the pan-Canadian Health Data Strategy, and is the Chair of the Alberta Virtual Care Working Group, and the National Health Data Roundtable. His current focus is health data governance and public policy, and is championing Stanford’s Collective Impact model as a transformative means to reimagine health data in Canada.

Dr. Affleck will be hosted by CHI on March 20th, 2024. If you would like to meet him or book some one-on-one time with him and your team please reach out to Erin.

AI4PH Summer Institute Applications OPEN

The Artificial Intelligence for Public Health (AI4PH) Summer Institute will be held in Calgary, Alberta in 2024. Successful applicants will have the opportunity to expand their skills and knowledge related to the application of AI innovations in public health research and practice while exploring the potential impact of AI technologies on the widening or narrowing of health inequities.
This year’s theme will be “Introduction to image analysis and applications to public health”, with methods and examples organized around this topic.
