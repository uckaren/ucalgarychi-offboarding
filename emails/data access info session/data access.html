The Centre for Health Informatics opened its doors to advance and innovate health informatics for Precision Health in the Cumming School of Medicine through timely data access, data analytics, linkage and coding. Through partnerships with AHS and other external collaborators, CHI is able to facilitate access to high quality, relevant data that can be used to support research and drive innovations.
